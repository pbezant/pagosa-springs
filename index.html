How to use this guide

Built for groups that don't all want the same thing.

Send anglers to the river, hikers up the trail, and meet back at the trailhead.

01

Open the fishing guide

Find your spot — organized by distance from town. Each card includes directions, species, access difficulty, and what flies to use. Spots with a 🥾 badge have a hiking trail at the same location.

02

Open the hiking guide

Browse trails by difficulty — from the flat Riverwalk to the Continental Divide. Use the elevation profile to compare trails at a glance. Trails with a 🎣 badge share a trailhead with a fishing spot.

03

Use the cross-references

The 🥾 and 🎣 badges link directly between pages. Anglers at the Piedra River can point hikers to the trail at the same trailhead. Turkey Creek, Williams Creek, and the Riverwalk all work this way.

04

Family-friendly picks

San Juan Riverwalk (flat, paved, stroller-friendly), Fourmile Falls (family moderate, spectacular waterfall), and the Piedra River family section (short canyon hike, great for ages 6+).
